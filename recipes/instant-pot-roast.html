How To Roast a Pot Roast Instantly Using This InstantPot Pot Roast Recipe.

ingredients :

  • a big chunk of meat.
  • some broth or water.
  • salt.
  • several carrots.

Instructions :

  1. put some salt on that meat.
  2. fry it in a pan with the butter that I forgot to add to the ingredients list.
  3. put the seared meat in the instant pot with the broth.
  4. set the instant pot to pressure cook for one hour and one half of an hour.
  5. take it out when its done and eat it with a fork.
